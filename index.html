About Me

Who's Behind This?


I'm Tiffany Mackins, a Charlotte-based Instructional and Web Designer with a passion for creating engaging and accessible digital experiences. With a background in both design and development, I specialize in crafting websites and learning modules that are not only visually appealing but also user-friendly and inclusive.

I thrive on collaborating with clients and teams to bring ideas to life and solve complex problems through design and development. Always eager to learn new skills and stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in the field, I am committed to delivering high-quality digital products that make a positive impact.
